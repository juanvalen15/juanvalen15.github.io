# [](#header-1)About

Hallo!, I am **Juan Valencia**. I am from **Buga**, **Colombia**, and I am working in The Netherlands as a **System Engineer** at the **Dutch Ophthalmic Research Center** and a **PhD Research Scholar** at the **Eindhoven University of Technology**.

For the past years I have moved to **Poland**, **Brazil**, **Germany**, where I have been working for my Bachelor's and Master's studies in Electrical Engineering. Right now, I am based in **The Netherlands** where I am pursuing my PhD with focus on **real-time embedded control systems** and working as a System Engineer with focus on **fluidics control for ophtalmic surgery**. I am passionate about engineering, sports, and meeting new people.

During my free time I like to sport (**swimming**, **running**, **cycling**), learn many new things (**reading**, **traveling**, **going to museum workshops**), and be creative (**playing the guitar or the ukulele**, **taking pictures**).
